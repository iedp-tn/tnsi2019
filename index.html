About Us

Entrepreneurship Development and Innovation Institute (EDII) is a not-for-profit society established by the State Government of Tamil Nadu in 2001, which is an apex organisation in the field of entrepreneurship education and self-employement promotion and innovations inthe State through various programmes. TNSI-2019 (Tamil Nadu Student Innovators) is a flagship programme which will identify student innovators and reward them to build a Minimum Viable Product to convert their ideas into a Start-Up. Tamil Nadu Student Innovators Award 2019 provides a state-wide opportunity for students to showcase their innovativeness and acquire expert knowledge & mentoring in a conducive environment. Tamil Nadu Student Innovators Award 2019 plans to conduct & challenge young minds in the form of programmes, Bootcamps & Hackathons to develop the spirit of entrepreneurship.
